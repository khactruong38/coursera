Sushi is a traditional dish and also a typical cultural feature of the country of the rising sun. Moreover, sushi has also crossed the border of Japan to become a favorite dish of many people in many countries.

Oriental Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish consisting of rice mixed with vinegar and other ingredients. Since ancient times, the Japanese have known how to incubate fish, shrimp and seafood in rice balls to keep the delicious taste.

Kawaii Sushi

Sushi has many different ingredients and recipes. However, the main indispensable ingredient is rice mixed with vinegar, combined with seafood ingredients called Tane, including: tuna, snapper, halibut, swordfish, grouper, eel, etc. ..
